## Managing Bookings

### Viewing Bookings

1. Click **Bookings** in the left sidebar
2. You'll see all customer booking requests
3. Use the filter tabs: All | Pending | Confirmed | Completed | Cancelled

### Approving a New Booking

When a customer submits a quote request:

1. Click on the booking to open details
2. Review the service requested, address, and price
3. Click **Approve** to confirm the booking
4. The customer will be notified and can proceed to payment

### Modifying a Booking

If you need to adjust the price or details:

1. Open the booking
2. Click **Modify Booking**
3. Change the necessary details
4. Add a note explaining the change
5. Click **Send to Customer**
6. The customer will receive the updated quote

### Assigning Staff

Once a booking is paid:

1. Open the booking
2. Click **Assign Staff**
3. Select the staff member(s)
4. Click **Confirm Assignment**
5. Staff will see the job in their portal

## Managing Your Team

### Adding a New Staff Member

1. Click **Staff** in the sidebar
2. Click **+ Add Staff**
3. Fill in their details:
   - Name
   - Email
   - Phone
4. Click **Create**
5. They'll receive login credentials

### Viewing Staff

The staff list shows:
- Name and contact info
- Current status (Active/Inactive)
- Number of assigned jobs

## Managing Services

### Editing Services

1. Click **Services** in the sidebar
2. Click on any service to edit:
   - Name and description
   - Base price
   - Estimated duration
3. Toggle **Active** to show/hide on website
4. Toggle **Popular** to feature it

### Adding a New Service

1. Click **+ Add Service**
2. Fill in the details
3. Click **Create**

Customers see these updates in real-time!
